Chocolate donuts

Ingredients:

  • 4 Cups of chocolate in piece.s
  • 3 Tablespoons of yeast.
  • 6 Tablespoons of butter.
  • 3 Egg units.
  • 1/2 Cup of sugar.
  • 1 1/2 Cups of milk.
  • 3 Cups of sifted wheat flour.
  • 1/2 Liter of edible oil.
  • White chocolate sparks (to decorate).
  • Sparks of milk chocolate (to decorate).
  • 1 Cup cocoa powder.

Steps to follow::

  1. Crumble the yeast, form a hole in the center with 1/4 of sugar, 1/2 cup of cocoa powder, 1 cup of flour and put the yeast in the center.
  2. Add 3/4 cup of warm milk so that the yeast dissolves.
  3. Incorporate the flour little by little and add 3 tablespoons of butter until you have a homogeneous mixture.
  4. Transfer the dough to a metal container and cover with a damp cloth, let stand for 30 min.
  5. Mix the rest of the flour, cocoa powder, eggs, butter, milk and sugar.
  6. When the volume of the dough that was left at rest increases, mix with the other. Leave in a metal container and stand for an hour or until doubled in size, cover with a damp cloth.
  7. Knead and use a rolling pin. Spread the dough until it is 2 or 3 cm thick. Cut in the shape of a donut, a large circle and a smaller circle in the center.
  8. Place the donuts in a tray and let stand for 30 min or until they double their volume.
  9. Fry the donuts in very hot oil and transfer to a dish with kitchen paper to drain the excess fat.
  10. Let cool for 5 min and melt the chocolate in a bain-marie.
  11. Dip each donut from the front into the melted chocolate.
  12. Serve
