Vanilla muffins

Ingredients

  • 4 Cups of flour.
  • 2 Cups of sugar.
  • 1 Tablespoon + 2 teaspoons baking powder.
  • 1 Teaspoon salt.
  • 2 Cups of milk.
  • 2 Large eggs.
  • 1 Tablespoon of vanilla extract.
  • 8 Tablespoons of melted butter.

Steps to follow

  1. Preheat the oven to 220ÂºC.
  2. In a large bowl combine the flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. In a small bowl beat eggs together with milk, melted butter and vanilla.
  3. Place the mixture of liquid ingredients in the dry ingredients, integrating and beating well. You have ready the mass of vanilla muffins.
  4. Now serve the dough in the muffin pans, which can be lined up in a common roasting pan, or you can use a special muffin pan.
  5. Bake for 20 minutes in the oven.
  6. Ready!
