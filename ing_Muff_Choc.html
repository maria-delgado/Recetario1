Chocolate muffins

Ingredients:

  • 300g Wheat flour.
  • 80g Pure cocoa, sugar-free.
  • And 1/2 Cdita. yeast chemistry.
  • 1/4 Cdita. Salt.
  • 250g Sugar.
  • 100g Butter.
  • 250ml Whole milk.
  • The juice of 1/2 lemon.
  • 2 Eggs.
  • 150g Chocolate 70% cocoa.

Preparation of chocolate muffins

  1. On the one hand we mix the solid ingredients, the flour, the cocoa, the yeast and the salt. We reserve In a bowl we mix the whole milk with the juice of half a lemon. Let stand for 15 minutes, so we will get a homemade buttermilk.
  2. Melt the butter in the microwave and reserve it. In a bowl we beat the eggs with the sugar. Mix well until you create a frothy cream. We add the melted butter and the buttermilk. We mix well.
  3. We incorporate the dry ingredients and, with some rods we mix until we integrate. It is not necessary that we work too much the mixture, it is only a matter of integrating the solids with the liquids.
  4. With a knife chop the chocolate 70% cocoa in small cubes. It does not matter if they remain irregular or of different sizes. We remove ¼ of the chocolate and the rest we add it to the mass of the Muffins. We remove to distribute them throughout the mass.

Baking and final presentation of chocolate muffins:

  1. Light the oven to preheat it to 190º C and while filling the capsules with the mixture, always leaving 1/3 of the free height.
  2. We have to take into account that the mass will rise so it is necessary that we leave a free space, without filling.
  3. We distribute the chopped chocolate we have set aside on all the muffins and bake for 20 minutes at 190ºC with heat above and below and without fan.
  4. Remove the Muffins tray from the oven and place them on a rack until they are completely cooled.
  5. The perfect thing is that we take them freshly made. Once they are warm, the chocolate chips will be melted in each bite. Imagine these Muffins accompanying a glass of milk can be a snack or a yummy breakfast.
