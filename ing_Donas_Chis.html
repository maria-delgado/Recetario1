Sparks donuts

Ingredients:

  • 40g Of fresh yeast.
  • 250g 0f sugar.
  • 80g 0f Butter.
  • 1k 0f flour.
  • 4 Egg.
  • 1 Vanilla Chorrito.
  • 200 Mililiters of milk.

Steps to follow::

  1. The first thing we will do is pour the flour into a bowl, in the center we make a hole to place the yeast.
  2. In the same hole we pour the milk as a thread, we reserve.
  3. We are going to melt the butter in the microwave and add it to the flour, then add the rest of the ingredients.
  4. Start kneading until you have a smooth mixture that can be stretched. It is important that its texture allows that it does not stick on our hands.
  5. Since our dough is ready, we are going to cover it and let it rest for an hour, this so that it takes volume.
  6. The indicated time has passed, and we are going to stretch it to break the gas bubbles, until it is only a few centimeters thick.
  7. When we have stretched the dough, we will cut the donuts, if you do not have a mold you can use any round object, a cup for example.
  8. When you have formed your donuts, it is important that we let them rest, some 30 minutes.
