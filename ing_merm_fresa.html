Strawberry jam

Ingredients

  • 5 Cups chopped strawberries.
    •                 
  • 3 Cups of sugar.
    •                 
  • 1/2 Teaspoon of pectin.

Steps to follow

  1. Mix the strawberries with the sugar in a bowl; allow them to macerate overnight.
    2.                 
  2. The next day, boil the mixture for 35 minutes, add the pectin and boil for five more minutes.
    3.             
  3. Turn off the flame and carefully pour immediately into sealed containers and boil for five minutes. Allow it to cool.
    4.                 
  4. Once one of the containers is opened, keep it in the refrigerator until it is consumed.
