Cakes mirror effect

Ingredients

  • 1 Cake, already made and which you like.
  • 2 Packages of unflavored gelatin.
  • 180 Cubic centimeters of water.
  • 200g Of sugar.
  • 200g Of glucose or corn syrup.
  • 150g Of sweetened condensed milk.
  • 200g Of chocolate.
  • Food coloring, quantity needed.

Steps to follow

  1. It dissolves the unflavored gelatin in 80 cubic centimeters of cold water and reserves.
  2. Boil the rest of the water with the sugar and glucose until everything is dissolved.
  3. Remove from heat and add gelatin.
  4. Add the condensed milk to the mixture.
  5. Pour the hot mixture over the chocolate and let it sit for 5 or 6 minutes.
  6. While you wait for it to take the right temperature and consistency, stir from time to time.
  7. Divide the mixture in containers according to the amount of colors you are going to use and keep them warm so they do not harden.
  8. Place the cake on a surface that is covered with a plastic film or similar to facilitate cleaning.
  9. Slowly spread the hot glaze over the cake. If you want to do it in several shades, do it quickly so you do not have to give it time to get hard and cover a larger amount of surface.
  10. Finally, put a spatula over the glaze to eliminate the excess and leave it as flat as possible.
  11. Let it sit before moving the mirror cake from its place.
  12. It may require refrigeration before being served.
