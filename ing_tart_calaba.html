Pumpkin pie

Ingredients

  • 200g Of pumpkin puree.
  • 75g Of wheat flour.
  • 50g Of butter without salt.
  • 50g Of brown sugar.
  • 2 Eggs.
  • 1 Yolk.
  • 25g Of sugar glass.
  • Half a tablespoon of cinnamon powder.
  • 100g Of milk cream.

Steps to follow

  1. The first step is to prepare the pumpkin puree, you just need to cut it into cubes and put a small amount of brown sugar, then bake at 180 ยบ C for 40 minutes, when ready liquefy or crush until the puree is formed.
  2. Next, mix the flour with the butter, which should be cold, the idea is to make crumbs and then add the sugar and mix.
  3. Then, add the egg yolk, mix everything to form a dough, which is wrapped with cling film, it is necessary to leave the dough in the refrigerator for 1 hour.
  4. Subsequently, grease a mold with butter, stretch the dough until it reaches a thickness of 3 mm and cover it with the mold. It is important to remove the excess of the dough and puncture or prick the base, this prevents bubbles from forming.
  5. Then the dough is covered with waxed paper and weight is added, they can be chickpeas for example, baked at 180ยบ C for 25 minutes, then the weight is removed and left in the oven for 5 more minutes.
  6. Meanwhile, the filling is prepared, for this the eggs are beaten with sugar and cinnamon, when a homogeneous product has been achieved, the cream of milk is added and everything is mixed very well. Finally, add the pumpkin puree prepared at the beginning and finish incorporating everything.
  7. At this point, it is time to take the oven for 35 minutes and at 170 ยบ C, it should be firm. Once it has cooled, it is removed from the mold with care and it is ready to be enjoyed.
