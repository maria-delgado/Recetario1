Grape jam

Ingredients

  • 300g Of isabella grapes.
  • 200g Of sugar (1 cup).
  • 1 Unit of Lemon.

Steps to follow

  1. Before making the grape marmalade, the first step is to prepare all the ingredients.
    2.                 
  2. In a pot add the washed grapes with peel, sugar and lemon juice. Mix everything very well.
    3.                 
  3. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly to undo the grapes.
    4.                 
  4. Continue cooking for about 20 minutes. We'll know that the jam is ready when we take a spatula and see the bottom of the pot.
    5.                 
  5. Finally serve the grape jam, wait for it to cool and enjoy.
