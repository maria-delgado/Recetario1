Peach jam

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2k Of peaches.
  • 1K Of sugar.
  • 1 Cup of water.
  • 1 Cinnamon slit.

Steps to follow

  1. Peel the peaches, remove the stones, cut them into quarters and place them in a layered container, alternating with sugar.
  2. Afterwards, let them marinate for 1 hour and cook on high heat for 10 minutes. Add water and cinnamon.
  3. Then continue cooking over low heat, stirring with a wooden spoon when it begins to thicken.
  4. When this thick, leave on fire for 10 minutes more and remove from heat. Let cool and serve.
