Walnut pie

Ingredients

    Dough

  • 1 1/4 Cup of wheat flour.
  • 4 1/2 Tablespoons butter without salt.
  • 1 Egg.
  • 1 Teaspoon of cold water.
  • 2 1/2 Tablespoons of refined sugar.
  • 1/4 Teaspoon of salt.
  • 1 Teaspoon of vanilla essence.

    • Walnut filling

  • 2 1/4 Peeled walnut cup finely chopped.
  • 3 Eggs.
  • 3 Tablespoons butter without melted salt.
  • 1 Spoonful of wheat flour.
  • 1/4 Of salt.
  • 1/3 Cup of brown sugar.
  • 1 Cup of maple syrup.
  • 1 Teaspoon vanilla essence or nut.
  • 2 Scoops of brandy (optional).
  • 2 Cups of extra nuts to decorate.

Steps to follow

  1. Place the flour in the blender, add the butter cut in medium cubes and beat until it has a sandy consistency
  2. Add the egg, sugar, salt, vanilla essence and teaspoon of water to the center; mix everything until obtaining a smooth and homogeneous mass.
  3. Cover the dough with kitchen plastic and refrigerate it for one hour.
  4. Beat the eggs for the filling lightly, add the maple syrup, the essence of walnut or vanilla, the melted butter, the brown sugar, the salt, the brandy, the flour and the finely chopped nuts; Beat until everything is well incorporated.
  5. Grease and flour a refractory or cake mold.
  6. Sprinkle flour on a clean work table, spread the refrigerated dough with the help of a rolling pin until it is the right size to cover the bottom and walls of the mold.
  7. Arrange the dough inside the mold and remove the excess. You can make a design on the edge pressing it with the fork.
  8. Chop the bottom of the dough with a fork, empty the filling over the dough and add the nuts to decorate; varnish the nuts to decorate with maple syrup.
  9. Cover the edges of the dough with aluminum foil and bake at 180 Â° for 50 minutes; Remove the aluminum from the edges and leave for five more minutes in the oven.
  10. Let the walnut pie cool at room temperature for four hours or overnight.
  11. Serve the perfect walnut pie and enjoy.
