Coconut donuts

Ingredients:

  • 1kg Of Wheat Flour.
  • 15g Or 3 tablespoons of yeast.
  • 150g Of sugar.
  • 20g Or 2 teaspoons of salt.
  • 150g Of butter.
  • 6 Eggs.
  • 1/2 Cup of honey maple.
  • 3 Tablespoons of vanilla.
  • 100ml Of water.
  • Wheat flour to extend.
  • 2 Liters of oil.
  • For icing: 2 cups of icing sugar.
  • 2 Lemons, just juice.
  • 1 Cup of water.
  • 2 Cups of grated coconut Utensils.
  • 1 Roller.
  • 1 Brush.
  • 18 cm Donut cutter.

Steps to follow:

  1. Make a source with the wheat flour and yeast, place sugar, salt, butter, eggs, honey and vanilla in the center.
  2. Make a premix with the ingredients of the center, add water and flour little by little; size until obtaining an integrated mass.
  3. Continue carving to give it elasticity; Watch the moment when the dough can be detached from the table and begin to hit it to smooth it. Remember that it should be soft, smooth and elastic.
  4. Let it sit for 1 hour or until it doubles in volume, lightly rub it with oil to avoid crusting, remove the air and turn the dough. Store it in refrigeration for 8 to 24 hours.
  5. Remove from the refrigerator and leave it a couple of minutes so that it is tempered slightly. Flour the table, extend in a rectangular shape and lightly varnish the surface with water and a brush.
