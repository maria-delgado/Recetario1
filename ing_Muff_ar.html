Rainbow muffins

Ingredients:

  • White cake mix.
  • 80ml Of water (1/3 cup).
  • 2 Tablespoons of oil.
  • 3 White eggs.
  • 4 Colorants for food.
  • Sweet and flavored sprinkles (optional).
  • Glazed.
  • Vanilla icing (white).
  • 4 Food coloring agents.

Steps:

  1. Stir the cake mix with water, egg white and oil in a large bowl. Stir until the mixture is smooth.
  2. Divide the mixture into 6 containers.
  3. Make the dough of different colors. Be sure to add at least 2 or 3 drops of food coloring to each of the containers, less of a lighter color, more of a dark color.
    • 1st container: red coloring
    • 2nd container: yellow coloring
    • 3rd container: blue coloring
    • 4th container: green coloring
    • 5th container: the same amount of red and blue dye to make purple dye.
    • 6th container: the same amount of red and yellow dye to make orange dye.
  4. Stir each container with a different spoon.
  5. Fill the muffin paper cup. Add the pasta in this order from the bottom up:
    • Purple (background)
    • Blue
    • or Green
    • Yellow
    • Orange
    • Red (upper)
  6. Bake the cupcakes at 325 ° F / 160 ° C for 20 minutes.
  7. Take 3 small containers and fill them with butter cream glaze.
  8. Put the red, yellow and blue frosting in a large plastic bag.
  9. Take the bag and cut a corner.
  10. Take the cupcakes and create a swirl effect with the frosting of the bags.
  11. You're done! Enjoy your rainbow muffins.
